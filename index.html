Kargil War Heroics

During the 1999 Kargil War, Captain Vikram Batra demonstrated unparalleled bravery, inspiring his team and leaving a lasting legacy.

Led the successful mission to capture Point 5140, displaying extraordinary courage under enemy fire.

Captured strategic Point 4875, saving his team and securing victory for India.

Famous for shouting "Yeh Dil Maange More!" during intense combat, symbolizing his indomitable spirit.

Martyred on 7th July 1999, posthumously awarded Param Vir Chakra for supreme bravery.
